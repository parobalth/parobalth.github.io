2016: Another year is over…

This blogpost is about some computer related things I did in 2016:

  • tested Freedombox on a Raspberry Pi 2
  • flashed OpenWrt to an old router
  • Installed Debian on my “new” old desktop Computer I could take home from work.
  • I preordered NextThings C.h.i.p. and wrote two posts about it.
  • backed Runcible kickstarter
  • backed EOMA68 kickstarter
  • wrote some GPG encrypted mail with FSF email bot Edward
  • I configured mutt and started to use it as console mail client.
  • configured RSS-feeds with tiny tiny rss on a shared hosting server and read them locally with newsbeuter.
  • GIF screencasts (I did some testing and the howto-post — but no GIF screencasts so far)
  • tested Tor browser on debian stable
  • switched to VimFX as a Pentadactyl replacement.
  • for testing purposes I started a journal (parobalth-journal) as a soup.io group but I am not really satisfied.
  • wrote some decent blogposts (for example: Note your last command, Vim: Run current line or Thoughts about NextThings C.h.i.p.)

To sum it up I believe it was a pretty good year hard- and software wise. I am quite happy what I managed to do this year. Everything else is cancelled and postponed for 2017. 2016 is over.
I still have a lot of drafts, ideas and (unfinished) projects for 2017. Stay tuned and Happy New Year!

If you have any questions, suggestions, thoughts and comments please feel free to email me.
